Long weekend updates:

  • Slightly improved startup time; UGWA also no longer should show the weird {{text}} while starting up. (By the way, those are for localization to support other languages.)
  • "Events" is now hidden during the brief startup stage to avoid being awkwardly left alone.
  • Left/right arrow keys now switch between days
  • There is now a message and buttons for when UGWA decides to intelligently jump to the next day to not confuse users.
  • UGWA now waits an hour after school before showing the next day's schedule, so parents can know when school ends.
