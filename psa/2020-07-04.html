UGWA has been updated for the 2020-21 school year.

Here's a tip for memorizing the schedule that might cease to exist on September 11th later this year:

  • All periods are 30 minutes on Monday and 75 minutes on other days!
  • Announcements infect the last numbered period before lunch and adds an extra 5 minutes! (Hint: SELF is not a numbered period.)
  • Days with SELF have brunch; other non-Mondays have breaks!
  • When in doubt, just remember that Thursday ends at 2:35 pm!

Because the schedule applies to all PAUSD secondary schools, Paly and middle schools can also use UGWA for time management and keeping track of assignments.
